Putcorn Time

Popcorn Time with put.io

What is Popcorn Time?

It's a brilliant mashup that brings together several API's to provide a catalog for watching torrents.

What is Put.io?

Put.io is a paid service that you can delegate your torrenting to.

You know where this is going, right?

With "Putcorn Time", instead of downloading torrents locally, you can utilize your put.io account. I have forked this application from the popcorntime.io repository and replaced the torrent client with put.io. Hope you like it
